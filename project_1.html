Project Overview

Over 80% of travellers spend 5 hours researching and planning a perfect trip.

According to 2023 Priceline Press study, over 80% of travelers dedicate at least five hours to researching and planning their ideal trip, highlighting the importance of thorough preparation in ensuring a seamless and enjoyable travel experience.

Main Goal

The main goal is to emphasize the significant time travelers invest in research and planning to ensure a smooth and enjoyable trip.

User Research

I conducted user research through focus groups with frequent travelers, both locals and foreigners, who travel in and out of Singapore. Their insights on needs and preferences shaped the design strategy, ensuring a user-friendly and seamless experience tailored to their travel planning process.

Target Audiences

  1. Frequent business travelers
  2. Family Travelers
  3. International Students
  4. Senior Travelers
  5. Digital Nomad

Competitive Advantage

Pain Points

  1. Busy Professionals
    • Lack of time to research and plan trips efficiently.
    • Overwhelming with travel options, making decision-making difficult.
    • Difficulty in coordinating schedules with work commitments.
  2. Travelers Who Like Customized Trips
    • Limited access to personalized recommendations.
    • Difficulty in finding unique or off-the-beaten-path experiences.
    • Hassle of planning multi-destination or activity-heavy trips.
  3. Families Seeking Budget-Friendly Trips
    • Struggle to find cost-effective travel options that fit family needs.
    • Hidden fees and unexpected costs when booking trips.
    • Limited availability of family-friendly accommodations and activities.
