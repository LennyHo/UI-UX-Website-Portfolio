Project Overview

How to create an app to store travel documents?

One of the biggest struggles for international travelers faces, is collating different types of travel documents in an efficient manner. It is also time-consuming tasks of gathering various types of travel documents in the digital era.

Main Goal

The main goal is to assist travelers who often for oversea work, foreign universities to use a faster convenient app to showcase digital documents before they arrive at the arrival checkpoints.

Imagine as a traveler...

  1. What information do you need to be aware when you have arrive my destination?
  2. What do you encounter when you provide your travel documents?
  3. How will you feel if you did not prepare or bring sufficient travel documents?
  4. How can I expedite the process as a traveler to clear at the immigration checkpoint?

Pain Points

  1. Time consuming to collate digital or physical documents
    Preparing and collating the necessary travel documents can be an incredibly time-consuming process. Travelers must gather both digital and physical documents, ensure that all paperwork is accurate and up-to-date, and align it with their flight schedules.
  2. Keeping themselves to update any changes of the visas
    Travelers may need to stay updated with frequent changes to visa regulations and requirements, which can be challenging and time-consuming. It also creates a significant stress and uncertainty for those planning international travel.
  3. Overwhelming Functionality of Travel Apps
    Travelers remain unaware of the full range of functionalities available in travel apps designed to manage and store travel documents. Many users are not familiar with these apps’ features or how they can streamline the organization of travel-related documents. This lack of awareness can lead to missed opportunities for simplifying travel planning and documentation management.
